Freelance and Corporate Projects (2019 - present)

Product Design Swift Playground – An accessible coding program for users who are blind:
Designed and built a prototype to help users who are blind code their bot's path through a maze. This prototype pairs the app with a tool that provides the user with audio and touch feedback – similar to a white cane – to help the user navigate the maze and test out their path code.

Instructional Design/Research Britannica for Parents — Ask the Expert Column:
Analyzed parent needs regarding how to support children with special needs during distance learning; wrote a column and designed a series of publicly available tools that address parents' pains, gains, and needs.

Product Design Sound City App — A Tangible Technology Phonics App:
Designed and developed [the front-end of] a multisensory phonics web application (for users ages 5-9) that utilizes tangible technology to provide users with activities that incorporate visual, auditory, and tactile learning.

Learning Experience Design Interactive Museum Exhibit Borders — A Borderless Experience of History:
Served as the design lead and co-developer of an interactive museum exhibit that explores the history of the U.S.-Mexico border. The design includes a number of interactive features: including murals containing hidden blacklight images (that visitors discover by using a UV Blacklight flashlight), and an app that allows users personalize their exhibit experience.
*Project Paused due to Covid-19.

Instructional Design Culturally Relevant Curriculum — Rap as Literacy and History:
Worked collaboratively with Northwestern University African Studies PhD students to design a multimedia curriculum:"Rap as Literacy and History", that employs culturally relevant pedagogy to create a strong user experience.
