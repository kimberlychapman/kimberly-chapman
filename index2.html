Experience
Freelance and Corporate Projects (2019 - present)

Product Design Swift Playground – An accessible coding program for users who are blind:
Designed a prototype of an accessible version of Apple's Swift Playground Coding app: where users write codes to move a character bot through a maze. To make this program accessible to users who are blind, this prototype pairs the app with a tangible tool — that provides the user with audio and haptic [touch] feedback as they navigate the maze and write/revise their code.

Instructional Design/Research Britannica for Parents — Ask the Expert Column:
Analyzed parent needs regarding how to support children with special needs during distance learning; wrote a column and designed a series of publicly available tools that address parents' pains, gains, and needs.

Product Design Sound City App — A Tangible Technology Phonics App:
Designed and developed a multisensory phonics web application (for users ages 5-9) that utilizes tangible technology to provide users with activities that incorporate visual, auditory, and tactile learning.

Learning Experience Design Interactive Museum Exhibit Borders — A Borderless Experience of History:
Lead a design team, from concept to execution, to create an interactive museum exhibit that explores the history of the U.S.-Mexico border: incorporating inclusive features for individuals with diverse learning/orthopedic needs, and an app that allows visitors to have a more personalized exhibit experience.

Instructional Design Culturally Relevant Curriculum — Rap as Literacy and History:
Worked collaboratively with Northwestern University African Studies PhD students to design a multimedia curriculum:"Rap as Literacy and History", that employs culturally relevant pedagogy to create a strong user experience.
